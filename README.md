# Rare-Earth-DCF-Strategic-Analysis
Rare Earth Strategic Analysis: MP Materials (NYSE: MP)
Project Overview
An integrated 5-year financial model and DCF valuation of MP Materials, evaluating the company’s strategic transition from rare earth oxide mining to vertically integrated magnet manufacturing. The analysis explores how government support and geopolitical positioning impact intrinsic value.

***Key Features*** <br>
Integrated Financial Model: A 5-year forecast projecting growth and margin expansion through 2030.

DCF Valuation: A Discounted Cash Flow analysis reconciling the gap between calculated fair value and current market pricing.

Strategic Analysis: Evaluation of the U.S. rare earth supply chain, focusing on the Mountain Pass facility and NdPr pricing dynamics.

Geopolitical Research: Conducted research to evaluate the company’s role in global trade partnerships and its impact as a domestic alternative to the international rare earth monopoly.

***Skills & Tools Used***<br>
Financial Modeling: Revenue forecasting, Free Cash Flow (FCF) analysis, and DCF valuation.

Strategic Research: Geopolitical risk assessment and supply chain analysis.

Software: Microsoft Excel.

***Key Findings***<br>
Analysis of major manufacturing expansions and their impact on long-term production costs.

Evaluation of the "Strategic Premium" driven by government-backed support and unique ownership structures.
